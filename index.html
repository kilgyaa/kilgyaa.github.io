Kílgyaa is an interactive dictionary of X̱aad Kíl (the Haida language). It is a work in progress.

Start typing to see dictionary entries automatically appear. Click on any entry to expand details including examples, inflections, and grammatical information.

Spelling conventions: You can enter text with or without accents and special characters (e.g. hlg̱ánggula and hlganggula will perform the same search). Use the orthography switcher above to toggle between Lachler and Classic orthographies.
